This Cookies Policy explains how cookies are used on the X Clone project. This website has been created solely for educational and portfolio purposes.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. They help remember preferences, improve performance, and enhance the browsing experience.

2. Cookies Used by This Project

At present, this X Clone does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies. No information is stored on your device while using this website.

3. Future Features

If user authentication, themes, or personalization features are added in future versions, cookies may be used to remember user preferences and maintain login sessions.

4. Third-Party Services

This project does not integrate advertising networks or third-party analytics services that place cookies on your device.

5. Managing Cookies

Modern web browsers allow users to control or disable cookies through their browser settings. Since this project currently does not use cookies, no action is required.

6. Policy Updates

This Cookies Policy may be updated whenever new features requiring cookies are introduced.

7. Contact
